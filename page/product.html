About

Surgicoll-Mesh®

Implantable, Bioresorbable Tissue Regenerative Sterile Type-I Collagen Matrix

Surgicoll-Mesh® is a Biocompatible Collagen Surgical Mesh in the form of a membrane that is supple, flexible, strong yet soft. when hydrated and handles like natural tissue; it readily conforms to the surgical site upon application and is easily sutured.

Surgicoll-Mesh® is a reconstituted Type-I Collagen mesh free of contaminants like lipids, elastin and other immunogenic proteins. The Collagen used in Surgicoll-Mesh® is based on patented technology that ensures high purity Type-I Collagen.

Surgicoll-Mesh® is Biocompatible and sterilized Type-I Collagen matrix for soft tissue & muscle flap reinforcement, third degree burns, surgical oncology reconstruction, dural repair, colorectal, urethral & vaginal prolapse, pelvic floor reconstruction and orthopaedic procedures like damaged tendon repair, osteochondral resurfacing, etc.

Why Surgicoll-Mesh®

Clinically Effective Through Dual Action Technology

Surgicoll-Mesh® delivers unmatched clinical performance by combining biocompatibility and bioactivity, a combination no other biomatrix can replicate.

Biocompatible
High-Purity Type-I Collagen

Made of native, un-crosslinked pure Type-I Collagen (>97% pure), completely free of immunogenic proteins, lipids, elastin, Type-III Collagen, and glycosaminoglycans. Parallel, organized fibers mimic native skin structure with ~20µm porosity ideal for cell infiltration and tissue regeneration.

Bioactive
Phosphorylated for Cell Signalling

The Type-I Collagen is phosphorylated, creating unique cell signal transduction capabilities. This attracts undifferentiated stem cells and converts them to specific cell lines for tissue repair. Improved cell signalling produces the molecules required for repair and regeneration a feature unique to Surgicoll-Mesh®.

Neo-vascularization Within 4–5 Days

Collagen phosphorylation attracts cells, regenerates tissue, and stimulates blood capillaries and granulation within just 4 to 5 days after application. This clinically proven rapid neo-vascularization is not shown with any other biomatrix product.

Ultra-Pure Extraction Process
Patented papain-based purification with reducing and delipidation agents yields >97% pure Type-I Collagen. This stringent process eliminates all immunogenic contaminants.
Native Parallel Fibril Architecture
Sedimentation process creates organized parallel fibers with ~20µm porosity matching native skin structure. Competing lyophilized products have random, non-native fibrillar structures with an average of >400µm porosity.
Phosphorylation — Enhanced Bioactivity
Type-I Collagen Charge modification through Phosphorylation leads to cell signal transduction; evidenced through a preclinical test documented at Stanford University, CA, USA which shows the attraction of neuronal cells towards the Phosphorylated Collagen. This exceptional bioactivity is exclusive to Surgicoll-Mesh®.
Ethylene Oxide Sterilization
An ideal sterilization process is used for Surgicoll-Mesh®. Unlike most other methods such as gamma and eBeam, ETO gas sterilization does not denature the protein. Each unit is individually sterilized in double-pouched packaging system
