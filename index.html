Surgicoll-Mesh®

Advanced Regenerative Mesh for Surgical Excellence

Surgicoll-Mesh® is a sterile, implantable, high-purity Type-I Collagen matrix engineered for soft tissue repair, reinforcement, and regenerative surgical applications. Flexible, strong, and easy to handle, it becomes supple upon hydration, conforms naturally to the surgical site, and can be sutured with ease.

Manufactured using Patented High Purity Type-I Collagen (>97%), Surgicoll-Mesh® is free from lipids, elastin, and other immunogenic contaminants. It is produced under stringent GMP conditions and undergoes rigorous USP quality testing in accordance with WHO and internationally recognized standards.

Surgicoll-Mesh® is intended for a broad range of reconstructive and regenerative procedures, including cartilage regeneration, soft tissue and muscle flap reinforcement, dural repair, oral and maxillofacial surgery, colorectal and urological reconstruction, pelvic floor repair, and burn wound reconstruction requiring skin graft support.

Who We Are!

Advanced Biotech Products (P) Ltd.
Advancing the Science of Tissue Regeneration & Wound Healing

Founded in August 1993 in Chennai, India, Advanced Biotech Products (P) Ltd. (ABP) is a pioneer in collagen biomaterials and regenerative medical technologies. Established by Dr. S. Gunasekaran, a globally recognized expert in collagen science, ABP is dedicated to advancing wound healing, tissue repair, regenerative surgery, and drug delivery through scientifically engineered biomaterials.
Today, ABP is a leading manufacturer of high-purity Type-I collagen medical devices, delivering innovative, biocompatible solutions designed to improve clinical outcomes and enhance patient care worldwide.
